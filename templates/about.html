About This Application

My name is Adiljan Abdurihim, Managing Consultant at Capgemini. I have created this application to help developers and businesses understand the current technology landscape by analyzing documents. The goal is to extract valuable insights about technology skills and trends that are relevant in today's market.

This application was created with the use of AI to provide insight into the current market and demand for technological stacks. The primary goal is to give developers a better understanding of what technologies are relevant in today's market, enabling them to make strategic decisions for upskilling and career development.

By analyzing PDF documents such as job descriptions, resumes, project requirements, and technical specifications, this tool extracts and tracks technology skills mentioned across all uploaded documents. This creates a comprehensive overview of technology trends based on real-world documents.
